Though the Willams pear is one of the most common types of pear, many may not know about its humble beginnings.

As early as 1765, the Williams Pear was founded in England by a schoolmaster, Mr Stair. Over time, it was introduced to the rest of the world. Now, you can frequently see it sold in many supermarkets!

Together with the woody aroma of cinnamon, star anise and cloves, its musky fragrance creates the Spiced Pear gelato. It's woody and fresh flavour will transport you away from the hustling and bustling life in Singapore to be one with nature.
