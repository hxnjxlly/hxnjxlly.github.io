One of the main ingredients we use to make this best selling gelato, Vanilla Fig, comes from the heart of French Polynesia, loctated in the Oceania continent.

This ingredient is Tahitensis vanilla, one of the most rare types of vanilla in the world, making up less than 1% of its production.

With its foral notes combined with marvellous swirls of balsamic figs, this sweet and fruity gelato is perfect to complement the hot Singapore weather!
